Less is the point.

An open-source Android launcher that replaces your home screen with something simpler. Text-first, zero animations, fully offline. Designed for e-ink displays, keypad phones, and anyone who wants less from their screen.

  • No animations, no smooth scrolling, no feeds
  • No internet permission - nothing leaves your device
  • Works with D-pad, T9, QWERTY, and touch
