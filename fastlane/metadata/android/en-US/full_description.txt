    enhanced features.</p>

<h2>Forked with Extra Features</h2>

<b>Features:</b>

- App Drawer Customization: Rename apps in the app drawer (home screen app renaming is already supported with a long
click).
- Advanced Gestures: A wide range of gestures, including taps and swipes, to trigger different actions.
- Clock Customization: Position the clock independently of the home apps, giving more control over your home screen
layout.
- App Drawer Layout: Change the alignment of apps in the app drawer independently of the home screen.
- Font Size Customization: Adjust the font size of sections of the home screen separately.
- Privacy Focused: Removed internet permission, ensuring your data remains private.
- Biometric Security: Lock settings behind biometric authentication (toggle option).
- Crash Reports: Ability to report crashes via email for better troubleshooting.
- App Usage Tracking: View app usage time directly from the home screen.

<b>Gestures Include:</b>

- Double Tap: Quickly tap twice to trigger a predefined action.
- Clock Tap: Tap the clock to access additional features or settings.
- Date Tap: Tap the date for easy access to the calendar.
- Usage Tap: Tap on the total usage to view system or app usage data.
- Short Swipes: Swipe up, down, left, or right to navigate or trigger different actions.
- Long Swipes: Swipe up, down, left, or right for additional gesture actions.

<b>Possible Actions Include:</b>

- Open Specified App: Instantly open any app of your choice with a gesture.
- Display App List: Show the full list of apps installed on the device.
- Lock Screen: Lock your device for security.
- Show Recently Opened Apps: Access recently used apps for quick multitasking.
- Open Notification Drawer: Trigger the notification drawer with a swipe or tap.
- Open Quick Settings: Access quick settings directly from the home screen.
- Power Dialog: Open power options like shutdown, restart, and more.
- Take a Screenshot: Capture the screen to save or share your content.
- Switch Page: Quickly switch between home screen pages.
- Disable Gesture: Disable specific gestures as needed.
